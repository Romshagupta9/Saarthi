Amazon Interview Preparation

Aptitude Questions

Q1: Find the product of first and last numbers in an arithmetic progression with mean 24 and common difference 4.
Q2: A shopkeeper sold two items, one at 25% profit and another at 5% loss. Find overall profit/loss percentage.
Q3: Probability of picking a multiple of 3 from numbers 1 to 250.
Q4: Find the next number in the series: 8, 15, 12, 19, 16, 23.
Q5: Find the width of the river using angle of depression values 30° and 45°.
Q6: What day will you attend the interview if today is Sunday and the interview is 4 days after the day before yesterday?
Q7: How many pencils are in the box based on given statements?
Q8: How is a person in a photograph related to Kiran?
Q9: Solve the mislabeled fruit boxes puzzle.
Q10: Find the missing letters in the series: bdf, hk, moq, ?

Q9: Flatten a strange linked list into a normal one.
